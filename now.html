WHAT I'M DOING NOW

Inspired by Derek Sivers and others on nownownow.com.

Updated on May 26, 2024, from my home in Auburn, Alabama.

I'm launching a book club!

After years of putting it off, I finally launched a weekly book club. It's an opportunity for me to talk about books and ideas that matter to me with people who are actively investing in themselves. We've got a private Discord channel, weekly meetings, and ongoing professional support. Things kick off next Thursday with "Linchpin" by Seth Godin. Want in? Send me an email.

I'm taking the Summer off from classes at Troy.

After two semesters with a 4.0 average, I'm taking the Summer to tackle a few items that need some devoted attention--that, and I'm playing a few games, reading a bit more, and learning how to create shell commands for Unix.

Here's what I'm reading now.

Right now, I'm in the middle of "Chain-Gang All-Stars" by Nana Kwame Adjei-Brenyah, and, man, is it one helluva book. Last year I did a bit of writing about the state of our prison systems. This book is about as honest and brutal a picture of our penal situation ass any I've ever encountered--and it's captivating. Every American should be reading this book.
