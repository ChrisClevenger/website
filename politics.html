Running against a Republican in the Deep South?

Uncontested.

In 2022, Democrats did not even field candidates against 24 Republicans for the U.S. House of Representatives. At the state level, the numbers get even worse. In Alabama, 64 incumbents ran unopposed—46 Republicans in the state House and Senate. The lack of competition from Democrats led to a Red Wave in Southern Legislatures where Republicans gained over 55 seats.

How can Democratic candidates be competitive in the South in 2024/2026?

For Democrats to be competitive in the South, they need to connect directly with voters, address issues that constituents care about, and mobilize communities to make a difference. The cheapest, most effective way to connect directly with voters is through SEO and content strategy.

  • Search Engine Optimization ensures voters are introduced to Democratic candidates when searching for information about issues.
  • By improving their ranking on popular search engines, candidates build authority and trust with their communities.
  • A thoughtful, content-driven website allows voters to educate themselves and make informed decisions.
  • A strong SEO and content strategy improves candidate visibility—-even among voters on the other side of the aisle.

SEO and Content Strategy are the best way for grassroots Democratic candidates to be competitive with Republicans online.
